# Marketplace Listing Draft

## Product Name

Real Estate Growth CRM

## Short Description

A ready-to-use CRM setup for real estate brokerages to capture leads, assign agents, manage properties, track site visits, and monitor deal pipeline.

## Long Description

Real Estate Growth CRM gives brokerages a structured Zoho-based operating system for property inquiries, agent follow-up, listings, site visits, bookings, and broker reporting.

Most real estate teams do not lose deals because they lack leads. They lose deals because inquiries arrive from many places, follow-up depends on memory, property matching is manual, and managers cannot see which leads or deals are stuck.

This vertical solution gives your team a practical CRM foundation built around the brokerage workflow:

- Buyer, seller, renter, landlord, investor, and referral lead tracking
- Property and listing management
- Site visit scheduling and outcome tracking
- Buyer/seller/rental deal pipelines
- Agent follow-up tasks and stale-lead visibility
- Booking and document-status tracking
- Commission visibility for managers
- Dashboards for brokers, agents, marketing, and operations

## Best For

- Real estate brokerages
- Real estate agencies
- Small and mid-size agent teams
- Teams using spreadsheets, phones, inboxes, and disconnected follow-up processes
- Broker owners who need better visibility into agent activity and pipeline movement

## Key Benefits

- Capture real estate leads in one CRM structure
- Assign every inquiry to an accountable agent
- Track buyer requirements and seller opportunities
- Connect deals with properties and site visits
- Reduce missed follow-ups with tasks and reminders
- Give brokers dashboards for stale leads, pipeline, source performance, and agent activity
- Create a reusable foundation for future integrations with forms, WhatsApp, SMS, finance, signatures, or portals

## Setup Notes For Customers

After installation:

1. Add users and assign roles.
2. Confirm profiles and access permissions.
3. Customize cities, areas, lead sources, and property types.
4. Import existing leads, contacts, and properties.
5. Review workflow rules and task due dates.
6. Add website forms or ad lead sources through your preferred Zoho integration path.
7. Train agents on lead follow-up, site visits, and deal updates.
