# Real Estate CRM Setup And Automation Package

## Package Positioning

This package helps real estate brokerages, agencies, property developers, and property managers turn Zoho into a practical operating system for leads, agents, listings, site visits, bookings, payments, commissions, documents, and reporting.

The pitch is not "buy a CRM." The pitch is:

> Stop losing property inquiries, agent follow-ups, booking opportunities, and management visibility because the sales process is scattered across WhatsApp, spreadsheets, portals, email, and manual reminders.

## Offer Name Options

Use one of these names depending on the buyer:

- Zoho Real Estate Growth System
- Real Estate Lead-to-Closing CRM Setup
- Zoho CRM Automation Package For Real Estate Teams
- Real Estate Sales Operations System With Zoho
- Zoho Property Lead And Agent Follow-Up Automation

Recommended default:

> Zoho Real Estate Growth System

## Best-Fit Buyers

This package is built for:

- Real estate brokerages with multiple agents
- Real estate agencies handling buyer, seller, renter, or investor leads
- Property developers selling units or projects
- Property managers handling owner, tenant, lease, renewal, and maintenance workflows
- Real estate teams using Zoho but still depending on spreadsheets, WhatsApp, manual reminders, and disconnected apps
- Businesses considering Zoho but unsure how it fits their real estate process

## Decision-Maker Hooks

### Owner, Founder, Broker, Or CEO

They care about revenue, leakage, team accountability, and visibility.

Message:

> You should be able to see which lead sources produce real opportunities, which agents follow up fast, which deals are stuck, and where revenue is leaking without chasing the team manually.

### Sales Manager Or Head Of Agents

They care about lead routing, follow-up discipline, agent activity, and pipeline quality.

Message:

> Every property inquiry should be assigned, followed up, tracked, and reported. No lead should disappear because ownership was unclear or follow-up depended on memory.

### Marketing Manager

They care about lead source ROI, campaign performance, and conversion tracking.

Message:

> Portal, ad, website, WhatsApp, and referral leads should all enter one CRM with source tracking, campaign reporting, and follow-up automation.

### Operations Manager

They care about handoffs, documents, approvals, site visits, and process consistency.

Message:

> Zoho can standardize the process from inquiry to site visit, offer, booking, document collection, payment, commission, and handover.

### Finance Or Admin Team

They care about payments, commissions, invoices, receipts, and reconciliation.

Message:

> CRM should not stop at lead follow-up. It should connect with payment, invoice, commission, and reporting workflows so finance is not rebuilding the truth manually.

## Core Business Problems This Solves

- Missed property inquiries from portals, ads, website forms, WhatsApp, calls, walk-ins, and referrals
- Slow follow-up after new leads arrive
- Unclear agent assignment and ownership
- Duplicate contacts, buyers, sellers, tenants, landlords, properties, or deals
- Messy pipeline stages that do not match real sales, rental, booking, or closing steps
- Site visits and showings tracked manually
- WhatsApp conversations disconnected from CRM history
- Documents, offers, bookings, agreements, and signatures handled outside the system
- Payments, receipts, invoices, and commissions disconnected from deal reporting
- Managers unable to see agent performance, campaign ROI, stuck deals, or forecast quality

## Automation Ideas That Create Buyer Interest

### New Property Inquiry Automation

Trigger:

- New lead from website, portal, ad, WhatsApp, call, or manual entry

Actions:

- Create lead in Zoho CRM
- Capture source, property interest, location, budget, and timeline
- Assign agent based on rules
- Create first follow-up task
- Send instant acknowledgment message
- Notify agent and sales manager
- Escalate if no activity happens within SLA

Why decision-makers care:

> This reduces missed leads and makes agent accountability visible.

### Agent No-Follow-Up Escalation

Trigger:

- Lead assigned but no call, message, or task completion within agreed time

Actions:

- Notify agent
- Notify sales manager after second delay
- Add lead to stale lead dashboard
- Optionally reassign after threshold

Why decision-makers care:

> Lead leakage becomes visible before revenue is lost.

### Property Match Automation

Trigger:

- Lead has budget, location, property type, and timeline filled

Actions:

- Suggest matching properties or inventory
- Create task for agent to send options
- Track matched property list
- Move stage to Property Matched

Why decision-makers care:

> Agents move faster and the business can measure demand by location, type, and budget.

### Site Visit Automation

Trigger:

- Site visit scheduled

Actions:

- Create calendar event
- Notify agent and lead
- Send reminder before visit
- Create post-visit follow-up task
- Capture visit outcome
- Update pipeline stage

Why decision-makers care:

> Management sees whether leads are becoming real appointments.

### Booking And Document Automation

Trigger:

- Deal moves to Booking Initiated

Actions:

- Generate document checklist
- Request missing documents
- Send agreement through Zoho Sign or DocuSign
- Notify finance/admin
- Update document status

Why decision-makers care:

> Deals do not stall because documents are scattered.

### Payment And Commission Automation

Trigger:

- Payment received, invoice created, or booking confirmed

Actions:

- Update CRM deal status
- Update payment stage
- Calculate or flag commission
- Notify finance and sales manager
- Update reporting dashboard

Why decision-makers care:

> Sales, finance, and management see the same revenue status.

### Lost Lead Reactivation

Trigger:

- Lead is marked lost, cold, no response, or not ready

Actions:

- Add to nurture sequence
- Schedule future follow-up
- Send relevant property updates
- Alert agent when lead re-engages

Why decision-makers care:

> Old leads become a future pipeline instead of a dead list.
